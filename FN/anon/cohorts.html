Peer Mentorship for Tech Startup Founders

At our core Founders Network offers peer advice based on experience. Members help each other with everything from general founder problems and moral support to specific technical issues and personal introductions to partners, investors and customers. This leads to better, faster and smarter decisions for your startup. Members meet online on our member site, offline through our events, and on their own through the relationships they created on Founders Network. We have a monthly member meeting where people meet each other face-to-face and build valuable long-term relationships, as well as happy hours where people network and share their startup experiences.

We currently have 150+ members. There are a mix of first time and serial founders with funding ranging from $0 - $50M. Though most of our members are located in the Silicon Valley area, over 25% live elsewhere in places like Australia, New Zealand, Spain, Mexico, Denmark, and China to just name a few. Our founders cover industries like Enterprise Software, Consumer Internet, Mobile, Gaming, Cleantech, Life Sciences and include companies like: Appthority - winner of the RSA Innovation Sandbox, 2012, Ifttt, BookRenter, ReTargeter, Iron.io, and InternMatch.
