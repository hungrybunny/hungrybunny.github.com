About Us

We are a private community of tech startup founders committed to helping each other succeed through peer mentorship. Members gain a support network featuring peer advice based on experience and personal introductions to key individuals like investors, customers and vetted service providers. The benefits are faster, smarter decision making; increased access to growth opportunities; and peace of mind from minimizing personal and management risk. Long-term we are fostering valuable relationships that provide an advantage for this startup and the next.

The Team

Kevin Holmes, Founder & CEO

Kevin started Founders Network to help tech founders achieve success through peer mentorship. Prior to Founders Network, Kevin advised hundreds of Silicon Valley entrepreneurs from idea stage through funding while managing Santa Clara University's entrepreneurial community as director of the Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE). Prior to CIE, he helped launch AllDorm.com, a national media and marketing firm that helped corporate and local brands connect with the college market. Kevin was named "40 Under 40 in Silicon Valley" by the Silicon Valley Business Journal for his work with startups and promoting entrepreneurship. He earned the Belotti Award from Santa Clara University's Graduate School of Business and has served on the adjunct faculty at both Santa Clara University Leavey School of Business and the University of San Francisco School of Business. Kevin holds a bachelor's degree and a Master of Business Administration from Santa Clara University.

Abby Crowley, Community Management Intern

Abby supports community management at Founders Network. Her areas of responsibility include coordinating member functions, managing the fnBlog and social media channels. In addition to her work at Founders Network, Abby is finishing a degree in Communications and Marketing at Chico State graduating in May '13.

Ryan Bennett, fnDeals Manager

Ryan coordinates fnDeals, group discounts on common startup expenses worth over $150k in savings. Ryan was previously founder of Liv, a consumer Internet startup similar to Pinterest that allowed users to collect items they "want, love, and Liv for". After Liv, Ryan joined and currently works at Idle Smart as vice president of Product and Marketing where he wears many hats but is primarily focused on creating new products from the ground up. He leads the engineering and design teams, oversees the user experience, and works with the rest of the team to create and implement the necessary strategies to make Idle Smart an exciting and successful venture. Ryan holds a BS in Entrepreneurship from Saint Louis University where he played varsity baseball.

Steve Salta, Business Development Manager

Steve manages business development for Founders Network. He is bi-lingual (Spanish/English) with over 9 years experience in management, business development, marketing and strategy. Prior to joining Founders Network, Steve managed international sales teams for networking software/hardware companies and more recently has been consulting for Latin American tech start-ups seeking to expand into the US market. Steve holds an MBA from the University of San Francisco and a BA in Spanish Studies and Communications from Santa Clara University.
