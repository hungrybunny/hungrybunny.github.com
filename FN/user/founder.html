Greetings. I've just transitioned to working full-time on my music tech venture Humix. Previously, I have worked for a handful of startups since graduating from SCU in '04, where my friends and I helped to introduce Entrepreneurial curriculum to the university.

Humix

Humix is developing a patented a system that sequences music playback cohesively and fluidly, using the same techniques of skilled DJs and music producers.

The result is that music playback sounds more smooth and enjoyable from one track to the next, like listening to an extended DJ set or a well-sequenced album from start to finish (think Dark Side of the Moon as one example). This enables users to listen to their own music longer and more enjoyably, and discover new music more easily by introducing new music in a harmonious way - effectively the same role as a skilled DJ playing familiar favorites, while placing new tracks in a crowd-pleasing, accessible manner.
