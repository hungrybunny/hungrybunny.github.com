Team Space isn’t just a virtual office: it’s a destination.

It’s a place to work, a place to share ideas and a place to connect with your co-workers. Designing your Team Space to fit your needs is the first step in building a successful team. Team Spaces come in different sizes, colors and configurations so that each team will feel comfortable at work. Currently we have six different sizes and styles to choose from. Once you sign up, you’ll be able to choose your size and your style.

Different Sizes

Team Space can accommodate any size team. Whether you’re small and agile or growing by the day, Team Space has the right size for you. Pick the size and layout that makes the most sense. Need more private offices? Want more meetings rooms? If you decide you need another size, no problem, you can swap it out easily.

Different Styles

You wouldn’t want to work in an unattractive physical office, would you? Why should your virtual office be any different? With that in mind, we offer a variety of styles designed to please a range of sensibilities, so that your team will feel at home. All of our styles have been designed to make Team Space easy to use and navigate.
